John Scalzi's Redshirts

Jul 15th, 2012

Just finished reading John Scalzi’s Redshirts. It’s funny, sometimes silly, but it’s also a page turner. I read it in a few hours, just could not put it down. And Scalzi manages to make something that looks like it’s obvious and then give it a twist and another and then another until you go, oh that’s the thing… It is definitely not hard scifi! I’d describe it more as a satyrical work making fun—albeit in a positive way!—of scifi. I loved it.

Oh, and it’s totally meta!
